CONVERSATIONAL AI PATENT

I have always been passionate about, and fascinated by, conversational AI. In 2018——recognizing its transformative potential——my VP of Product, Tyler Forbes Cook, and I sought innovative ways to push the boundaries of conversational AI technology. Together, we designed and developed a cutting-edge conversational AI shopping platform. We partnered with Google at the time and worked to help train their LLM that would be the start of what has now grown into Google's Gemeni today.

As pioneers in the era of Siri, Amazon Alexa, and Google Assistant, our work led the company to patent these innovations, solidifying our contributions to AI-driven commerce.

I still have a pation for Conversational and Agentic AI and I'm so excited for the challenges and opportunities the future of AI presents.
